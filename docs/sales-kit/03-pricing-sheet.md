# Pricing Sheet

## Pricing Principles

Use these rules when presenting pricing:
- Setup fee is required because KB quality decides AI quality.
- Monthly pricing should reflect business value, not only token cost.
- Usage is counted as AI replies, not vague "sentences".
- WhatsApp / Meta pass-through charges may be billed separately if applicable.
- Max should use fair-use wording until high-volume ops are mature.

## Starter

For small businesses that want to try AI booking with no monthly commitment after setup.

```text
HK$998 setup
HK$0/month after setup
150 AI replies/month
1 WhatsApp account
Limited dashboard: bookings + customer information
Assisted setup included
```

Sales wording:

> Starter is for shops that want to test AI reception without a monthly plan. You still pay setup because we need to build and test your business knowledge base properly.

## Founder Offer

Recommended:

```text
First 30 pilot customers can access selected Max features at Pro price for the first 3 months.
```

Alternative:

```text
First 100 customers lock founder pricing for 12 months.
```

Avoid:

```text
Free Max forever.
```

Reason:
- It weakens Max pricing.
- It can create support load before the Max workflow is mature.
- It makes future upgrades harder to explain.

## Usage Language

Say:

> Usage is counted by AI replies. If one customer booking takes about 5 AI replies, 150 replies can support roughly 30 booking conversations.

Avoid saying:

```text
150 sentences
unlimited conversations
```

Use:

```text
AI replies
fair-use high volume
```

## Upgrade Path

Starter -> Pro:
- More usage.
- More WhatsApp accounts.
- Full dashboard.
- Better for active operations.

Pro -> Max:
- Higher volume.
- More WhatsApp accounts.
- Custom chatbot tuning.
- Business insight layer.
- Human intervention workflow.

## Add-Ons For Later

Do not include these by default in MVP pricing:
- Calendar integration.
- Payment integration.
- Custom CRM integration.
- Advanced analytics.
- Multi-branch enterprise reporting.
- Custom data migration.

Quote separately when requested.
