# Objection Handling

## "會唔會亂答價錢？"

Short answer:

> The AI only quotes prices from your approved service list. If the price is missing, it should say staff can help check instead of inventing.

Longer answer:

> We build your knowledge base first: services, prices, FAQs, opening hours, and booking rules. The AI is instructed to use only those facts. We also test unknown-service questions before go-live.

## "如果 AI 答錯點算？"

Answer:

> First, we reduce risk by keeping the AI grounded to your approved KB. Second, we keep staff handoff available. Third, we review real conversations during the pilot and improve the KB.

## "我唔想完全交俾 AI。"

Answer:

> You do not have to. MVP is AI receptionist plus human handoff. It handles repeated questions and booking collection, while staff can still intervene.

## "可唔可以接真人？"

Answer:

> Yes. If the customer asks something outside the KB, or the request needs staff judgement, the AI can hand off.

## "會唔會直接幫客落錯 booking？"

Answer:

> The booking flow requires confirmation. The AI collects service, date, time, name, and phone, then summarizes before creating or changing a booking.

## "改期取消得唔得？"

Answer:

> Yes. The AI can handle reschedule and cancellation, but it confirms before making the change.

## "Setup 係咪好麻煩？"

Answer:

> We do assisted setup. You send your service list, prices, FAQs, opening hours, and booking rules. We import and test it for you.

## "點解要 setup fee？"

Answer:

> The setup fee pays for building and testing your business knowledge base. AI quality depends on accurate services, prices, FAQs, and booking rules. Without setup, the AI is more likely to answer poorly.

## "點解 Starter 免費月費都要 HK$998？"

Answer:

> Starter has no monthly platform fee after setup, but setup is still needed. We need to configure your services, prices, FAQs, and booking flow properly before customers use it.

## "你哋同普通 chatbot 有咩分別？"

Answer:

> This is not just a FAQ bot. It is focused on appointment businesses: service questions, price answers, booking collection, confirmation, reschedule, cancellation, and dashboard visibility.

## "Dashboard 有咩用？"

Answer:

> For MVP, dashboard lets you see bookings, customer information, conversations, and KB. The main value is still the AI helping you reply and capture bookings.

## "可唔可以駁我而家個 calendar / POS / CRM？"

Answer:

> Not in the basic MVP package. We can discuss it as a separate integration after the pilot proves the booking flow and message volume.

## "WhatsApp 官方費用點計？"

Answer:

> Platform pricing covers the AI service. WhatsApp / Meta template or messaging fees, if any, may be pass-through or billed separately. We will clarify before go-live.

## "會唔會好貴？"

Answer:

> The question is how many enquiries and bookings you miss today. If AI helps recover even a few bookings a month, it can cover the monthly fee.

Example:

```text
If one booking is worth HK$500,
recovering 3 bookings/month = HK$1,500 revenue.
That already covers Pro's regular monthly fee.
```

## "我哋唔係好多 enquiry。"

Answer:

> Then Starter may be enough. You can start with lower usage and upgrade only when message volume grows.

## "我驚客人知道係 AI 會唔鍾意。"

Answer:

> The AI should be helpful, short, and practical. Most customers care more about getting a fast answer and a confirmed booking than whether the first reply came from staff or AI.

## "準備資料要幾耐？"

Answer:

> If your service and price list is ready, simple setup can usually be prepared in 1-3 working days.

## "有冇保證多幾多 booking？"

Answer:

> We do not guarantee revenue. What we can prove in the pilot is faster replies, fewer missed enquiries, more consistent answers, and a working booking capture flow.

## Closing Question

Use this to move the conversation forward:

> If we set this up with your actual services and prices, would you be open to testing it with real enquiries for one month?
