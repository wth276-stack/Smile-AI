24/7 AI WhatsApp receptionist for appointment-based service businesses.

## What It Does

AI TOP SALES helps service businesses reply faster, reduce missed enquiries, and collect bookings automatically.

It can:
- Answer common WhatsApp / webchat questions.
- Quote service prices from your approved service list.
- Recommend relevant services based on customer needs.
- Collect booking details: service, date, time, name, phone.

- Hand off to staff when the AI should not answer.
